FAQ

What taxes does TaxC calculate?

Personal Income Tax (PIT) based on 2025/2026 NTA rules for salary, business, rental, and investment income.

Who pays income tax?

Anyone earning over ₦800,000 annually after deductions must pay to their State IRS.

Rent & Pension deductions?

20% of rent (max ₦500,000) + pension contributions reduce your taxable income.

What about WHT?

10% for rental/investment, 5% for other income—credited against your final tax.
