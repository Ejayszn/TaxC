Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously at TaxC

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum data needed to provide you with a fast, secure, and personalised experience:

  • Full name or first name – to address you properly in the app and emails.
  • Email address – required for account creation, passwordless login, purchase receipts, and important notifications
  • Account creation & last login timestamps – for security monitoring and protecting your account
  • Purchase history – which eBooks and tax reports you bought, date, amount, and download links (so you can re-download anytime)
  • Email verification status – whether you have clicked the verification link we sent
  • Policy & Terms acceptance versions – record that you accepted the current Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

We do NOT collect or store:
Phone numbers · BVN · Bank accounts or card details · Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) · Home or office address · Date of birth · Any payment information (all payments are processed securely by Paystack, we never see your card).

2. How We Use Your Data

We only use your information to:

  • Create and secure your TaxC account
  • Send you passwordless login links and account-related emails
  • Deliver the eBooks and tax reports you purchased
  • Let you access your library from any device or browser
  • Notify you about new products or important service updates (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All your data is securely stored on Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (HTTPS + Google-managed keys)
  • Strict access controls, only automated systems can read your data when needed to deliver the service
  • Regular backups and security monitoring
  • Full compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and Google’s ISO 27001, SOC 2/3 certifications

4. Your Rights Under NDPR

You are in full control of your personal data:

  • Right to access – see all data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification – correct any inaccurate information
  • Right to deletion – permanently delete your account and all data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, just send an email to support@taxc.com.ng, we’ll respond within 48 hours (usually much faster).

By using TaxC, you agree to this Privacy Policy. For the rules governing the use of the service, please read our Terms of Service.
