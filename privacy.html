Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously at TaxC

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data strictly necessary to deliver and improve our services:

  • Your name – to personalise your account
  • Your email address – for account creation, login, and communication
  • Purchase history – records of all eBooks and detailed PDF tax reports you have bought (including titles, dates, and amounts)
  • Last login timestamp – for security and account protection
  • Saved calculator inputs – only if you are logged in and choose to save them

We do NOT collect phone numbers, BVN, bank account details, tax ID numbers (TIN), home address, or any payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Let you access your purchased eBooks across devices
  • Secure your account and prevent unauthorized access

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is securely stored on Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with:

  • End-to-end encryption at rest and in transit
  • Compliance with Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

4. Your Rights Under NDPR

You have full control over your data:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification or correction
  • Right to deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at: support@taxc.com.ng
